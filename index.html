SUB DISTRICT HOSPITAL SELU(SDHSELU)

“TO MAKE A DIFFRENCE IN PEOPLES'S LIVES THROUGH EXCELLENT PATIENT CARE”


We are one of the leading multi-disciplinary public healthcare service providers in Marathwada Region. We offer a comprehensive bouquet of healthcare service across specialties and super specialties through our Government recognised national programmes. we have a facility that can accomodate 50+ beds and a diverse range of multl-speciality Doctors.

Starting with basic needs of clients coming for treatment. Like providing safe drinking water, cleanliness inside and outside of hospital. Providing 24 hour electricity,Fan,Light, clean bedsheets,blankets,pillow,and other regular needs of clients. We focus on curative services and started focus on maternal and child health services. From Month of May2017 we started giving Caesarian section and hysterectomy services at our institute with help of Dr.Sharad Wagh Gyanecologist.

We provide curative surgical services like:
Caeserian section
Hysterectomy
Tubectomy

We started with RBSK surgeries also and in this surgery we operated with patients of hernia,phimosis,toungue tie, hydrocoele,sebaceous cyst, undecended testes,warts,fibroid adenoma ,corn,lipoma,prefacial adhesions,appendectomy and ganglion in school going students.

We offer a better Bio-medical waste management in hospital which is done abiding the safety standards which include a yearly contract with Atul environmental services for daily collection of waste and disposal at their site.there is a proper display of Bio-medical waste segrigation waste posters at genral ward,labour room ,operation theater etc.

We conduct two immunization sessions per week at sub-district hospital selu,one on wednesday and another on saturday every week also such sessions are conducted at diffrent places in selu city to cover the maximum beneficiaries.We have successfull ANC clinic ,NBSU ward ,ARSH clinic ,Family planning operations,blood bank,RBSK programme ,MAITREE clinic ,ICTC centre for patient care.

We have a successful Orthopedic clinic conducting varoius free camps. AYUSH facility has started in selu wherein Ayuveda,Homeopethy,unani adn yoga services are provided to the patients ,several camps have been arranged till date for the patients benefit.

National programmes like NLEP,Tobacco Control,Programmes for Tuberclosis,Programme for Vertor borne diseases NVBBCP,National Blindness control programme etc are being conducted at regular intervals every year.

Benefits of JSY and JSSK services are bieng regularly provided.Auxillary services like free food for pregnant ladies and their families is provided ,successful laboratory services are given,authentic blood bank with govt. registration is funtioning at the hospital.

Number of deliveries increase and total normal deliveries till now – 2461.

By this service we were awarded Dr.Anandibai Gopalrao Joshi Award twice and Kayakalp Award once. Nagar Parishad Selu Awarded our Medical Superitendent as the Best Officer in Selu . We developed a beautiful Garden in our premises with contribution of all Staff in SDH. Many clients after their satisfaction are giving donations in the form of Cots,Bucket,Fan,Stationary,Mops,Air conditioner and Child park benches. We arrange multi diagnostics camps ,ophthalmic camps ,blood donation camp,AYUSH camp in the institute and provide good services in these camps. Regular handicap certificate distribution service is given on every first and third Saturaday in our institiute. We arranged Mahila melava and haldi kunku programme on the occasion of makarsankranti. For all these services our doctors, incharge sister ,staff nurse, office staff, servant,sweeper,driver and security worked hard.

We strive for perfection in public health care and will continue to do so.
